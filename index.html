Best Day

2018-02-21

到家后第一时间坐到这里打开电脑打开WPS，甚至没有先拆快递，就想赶紧记录下这一刻的心情。

今天是2018年到现在最开心最快乐的一天了，可以和2017年考过专八、考过托福、考过语言课期中考试并列变成几大开心日之一23333，对于一个活在虚幻世界里的人来说，现实中这么快乐似乎只有考试成绩了，我没想到会因为一个人让我这么开心，因为我基本和所有人都无法特别热络。今天开心是因为我找到了失散多年的朋友！！闺蜜这个词远远不能表达我和她之间的关系，我和她自从高中毕业就断了联络，她当时甚至没有自己的手机，找她都要打她爸的电话，所以自然微信QQ这种东西都没有，换过几次手机和手机号的我谁的联系方式都没有，只有身边必需的同学亲友。大学时我去她妈妈单位找过一次，走了好几圈都没有找到QAQ然后就放弃了，前几天过年，高中同学群里有人发红包，我才发现我们有个高中群= =我在出国之后和一个女生聊得很好，所以问了她哪个是她，才加到了她的微信，在高中毕业后我没有去高中同学聚会。

Jane Eyre

2017-12-20

Lech asked us to give a presentation about a famous person after vacation. I chose Charlotte Bronte. I remember her in the English literature classes. I remember a lot of people actually. I was actually enjoying those days preparing for the English and American literature exams. Ann told me she would talk about Allan Poe because he has a very short lifetime and his life story would be easier to present. Ann gave me this idea to choose a writer for presentation’s topic because it will be easy. I spent a lot of time searching for some famous person who is not a writer and easy to talk about. And I got nothing… Ann seems to know a lot about those writers because her major is English and American literature. It is such a strange feeling. I just wonder does she love those writers and sympathize with them like I do. I just wonder does the girl my loved one marry love him and know how great he is like I do.

First day

2017-11-16

I have been constantly worrying that my personality is not suitable for going abroad. Everyone told me I have to be very enthusiastic and to take the initiative to start a conversation here，to make more friends，to be extroverted. After a couple of days being anxious and afraid，I figured it out，that I can not change to a different person overnight. That’s just not who I am. I can improve my English skills the other way. If that doesn’t work，then I will just give it up. If I can change myself so easily, we won’t end up here…
