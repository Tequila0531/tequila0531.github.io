First day

Nov 16, 2017 | Diary | 阅读 | 本文共计 328 字 | 阅读约需 2 分钟

I have been constantly worrying that my personality is not suitable for going abroad. Everyone told me I have to be very enthusiastic and to take the initiative to start a conversation here，to make more friends，to be extroverted. After a couple of days being anxious and afraid，I figured it out，that I can not change to a different person overnight. That’s just not who I am. I can improve my English skills the other way. If that doesn’t work，then I will just give it up. If I can change myself so easily, we won’t end up here…

I don’t know how to descibe recent days. I want to say bitter and sweet, but actually it’s just bitter and bitterer. First day here, I was completely collapsed. A mental meltdown. I remembered that my hands were so cold all the day. Like I was going to give a presentation…I couldn’t sleep at night. There are like 100 problems that need me to handle.

After first few days, I was better, mentally. However, I was very sick…I got a fever and coughed my lungs out. And there’s more, my moblie was left at the train station and I spent an hour to find it. Luckily I found it. It was picked up by a nice person. And the next day in the wildlife park, I broke it…It fell down to the ground so hard that I thought I was dreaming.

Guess I was never lucky:)

And now, the course has been started for four days, I am like the worst student…My paper was marked by the teacher and to be honest, I haven’t seen this huge number of mistakes my whole life. One thing I am really good at turns to be my worst nightmare. I just wanna go home. You will be amazed how fast I can pack my luggage and fly home.

rot in hell
